## Kill Criteria

**Kill a track when:**
- Zero signal after 2 full test cycles (4 posts, 0 keywords, 0 DMs)
- Cost per lead exceeds $50 with no conversion path visible
- The deal doesn't pencil after due diligence (AI-proof score < 60, net < $8K/month)
- A module takes 3+ weeks to build with no user validation underway
- The founding assumption is proven wrong by data (not just inconvenient data — definitively wrong)

**Kill a deal when:**
- Operator dependency score is high and can't be systematised within 90 days
- Revenue is declining MoM for 3+ months with no clear explanation
- Multiple exceeds 40x monthly net without a growth catalyst
- Legal or compliance risk is unquantifiable
- Seller is evasive on traffic source or churn data

---

## Fuel Criteria

**Double down on a track when:**
- 3+ keyword comments within 48 hours on a test post
- First 5 early access signups arrive within 24 hours of going live
- A deal has AI-proof score 80+, net $12K+/month, asking multiple under 30x
- An agency client closes within 30 days of first contact
- EVA module saves Vineet 2+ hours/day — ship it and monetise it
- Any content piece generates 5+ inbound DMs from target persona in 48 hours
